- **Main Element:** Modern, glowing AI brain or neural network visualization

- **Text:** "NOVUS" in large, bold white text (Inter or similar modern font)
- **Subtitle:** "AI Prompt Optimization Agent" in smaller white text

### Design Style
- **Aesthetic:** Apple-quality, professional, tech-forward
- **Layout:** Clean, modern, with clear visual hierarchy
- **Effects:** Subtle blue/purple glow effects on key elements
- **Typography:** Modern, readable fonts with good contrast
- **Mood:** Professional, trustworthy, innovative

- **Transformation:** "Vague → Precise" or "Transform Ideas"
- **Value:** "$29 Lifetime Access" clearly visible
- **Professional:** Clean, modern design that builds trust

### Technical Requirements
- **Dimensions:** 600x600 pixels (square)
- **Format:** High-quality PNG or JPG
- **Readability:** All text must be clear at thumbnail size
