## 🎬 Technical Notes

### Recording Setup:
- **Screen Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps minimum
- **Audio Quality:** Clear voice recording
- **Background Music:** Subtle, professional

### Editing Elements:
- **Captions:** Include for accessibility
- **Transitions:** Smooth between sections
- **Graphics:** Professional, consistent branding
- **Call-to-Action:** Clear and prominent

### Optimization Tips:
- **Keep it under 60 seconds** - Attention spans are short
- **Show, don't tell** - Visual demonstrations work better
- **Include social proof** - Customer testimonials build trust
- **Clear CTA** - Make the next step obvious
