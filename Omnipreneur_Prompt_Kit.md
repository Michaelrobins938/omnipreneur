### **🚀 Model-Specific Prompt Arsenal**

#### **Claude 3 Opus - Your Strategic Brain**
*Best for: Deep analysis, complex reasoning, and nuanced understanding*

**Temperature**: 0.3-0.5
**Persona**: The wise strategist who sees patterns others miss

**Market Research Prompt**:
```
You are a master market analyst with 20 years of experience in digital business. Your specialty is identifying untapped opportunities in saturated markets.

TASK: Analyze the [YOUR NICHE] market and identify 3-5 micro-niches with:
- Low competition but high search volume
- Clear pain points that people will pay to solve
- Scalable business models
- Realistic entry barriers

FORMAT YOUR RESPONSE AS:
1. Market Overview (2-3 sentences)
2. Top 5 Micro-Niches (with competition level, search volume, and monetization potential)
3. Entry Strategy for Each Niche
4. Risk Assessment
5. 90-Day Action Plan

NICHE: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
TARGET AUDIENCE: [INSERT YOUR AUDIENCE]
BUDGET: [INSERT YOUR BUDGET]
```

**Product Ideation Prompt**:
```
You are a product development expert who has launched 50+ successful digital products. You understand what makes people buy.

TASK: Generate 10 digital product ideas for [YOUR NICHE] that:
- Solve a specific, painful problem
- Can be created in 30 days or less
- Have multiple revenue streams
- Scale without proportional effort

FOR EACH IDEA, PROVIDE:
- Product name and core concept
- Target audience and pain point
- Revenue model (pricing strategy)
- Creation timeline
- Marketing angle
- Risk level (1-10)

NICHE: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
SKILL SET: [INSERT YOUR SKILLS]
TIMELINE: [INSERT YOUR TIMELINE]
```

### **🎛️ Advanced Configuration Settings**

#### **Temperature Guidelines**
- **0.1-0.3**: Factual, consistent, predictable (use for technical tasks)
- **0.4-0.6**: Balanced, creative but focused (use for most business tasks)
- **0.7-0.9**: Creative, diverse, innovative (use for ideation and content)

#### **Behavioral Tuning**
- **Claude**: Add "Think step by step" for complex reasoning
- **GPT-4**: Add "Be creative and unexpected" for ideation
- **Command R+**: Add "Be precise and actionable" for execution
- **Mistral**: Add "Consider future implications" for trend analysis
